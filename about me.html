About

Hello my name is Dean. I have been drawing since I could remember. 
My inspiration has been Takato Yamamoto (I have a piece tattooed on me) and many online artists that I have followed throughout the years. I have been taking all available art classes since highschool, I have picked up many mediums from hands on to digital work. My life without drawing is consisant of playing video games and reading manga or novels.                  

My favorite media is digital, I use my Ipad for my tool. Although I would say my best work has been painting. Digital is more convient and on the go while traditional seems a lot to tackle on, my kitchen would be a mess every project when I was taking my Elements of Design class! 

 I have touched on character design by making my DnD character into a full fleshed out character with a background and timeline. I also made a partner for him named Amon, making sure he had as complex background as the first character.

My biggest passion in making life is to create. It doesn't necessarily mean only art. Humans create memories, love, and lives. Everyone is an artist one way or another and I truly believe  that from the bottom of my heart.
